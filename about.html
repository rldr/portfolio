Hi, I'm Rochelle.

I design intuitive solutions to make life simpler.

I embarked on my UX journey in 2017, immediately after finishing my senior year of college at UCLA. However, my passion for design did not start there. Growing up, I was a true creative at heart—competing in art competitions, teaching myself new crafting skills, and designing school merchandise and banners. During college, I discovered the vast amount of opportunities in the ever-evolving world of technology. As a computer science major, I started teaching web design to kids as a part-time job. I figured that the realm of tech was a viable path for me, but I wanted to be able to fuel my love of visual art at the same time. When choosing my career path, I sought to merge the two seemingly incompatible passions of mine and happily landed in the world of user experience.

As a designer, I follow a simple equation: beautiful interface + splendid experience. I believe that technology should act as a catalyst for a fulfilling life, and that’s exactly what I strive to help you achieve. So, feel free to take a peek at what I’ve been working on, and do let me know how we can work together. Thanks for visiting!
